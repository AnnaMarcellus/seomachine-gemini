## Linking Strategy by Content Type

### Product-Focused Posts
- Link to: Product pages, features, pricing
- Frequency: 3-5 internal links per post

### Educational Posts
- Link to: Related guides, pillar pages, resources
- Frequency: 3-5 internal links per post

### Comparison Posts
- Link to: Product pages, case studies, alternatives
- Frequency: 5-7 internal links per post

## Anchor Text Guidelines

### Good Examples
- "our [product] helps you..."
- "using [feature], you can..."
- "learn more about [topic]"
- "in our guide to [topic]"

### Avoid
- "click here"
- "read more"
- "this page"
- Exact match over-optimization

## Link Placement Best Practices

1. **First 25% of content**: Link to pillar page
2. **Middle 50%**: Link to related articles
3. **Last 25%**: Link to conversion pages
4. **Contextual**: Links should fit naturally

## Pages to Avoid Linking To

- Outdated content
- Low-value pages
- Thin content
- Pages outside your domain

## Update Schedule

- **Monthly**: Add new published content
- **Quarterly**: Review and update anchor text
- **Annually**: Audit linking strategy effectiveness
