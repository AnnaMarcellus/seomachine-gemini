## Article Requirements

### Length & Structure
- **2000-3000+ words** (based on SERP analysis)
- **4-7 H2 sections** with logical progression
- **H3 subsections** where needed for depth
- **Short paragraphs**: 2-4 sentences max

### SEO Requirements
- **Primary keyword** in:
  - H1 headline
  - First 100 words
  - 2-3 H2 headings
  - Conclusion
- **Keyword density**: 1-2% (natural, not forced)
- **Internal links**: 3-5+ to relevant pages
- **External links**: 2-3+ authoritative sources

### The Hook (CRITICAL)

**NEVER** start with:
- "X is..." definitions
- "When it comes to..."
- "In today's world..."

**ALWAYS** use one of these hook types:

| Type | Example |
|------|---------|
| Provocative Question | "What if the 'free' plan is actually costing you $500/month in lost opportunities?" |
| Specific Scenario | "Last Tuesday, Sarah checked her dashboard and discovered something alarming..." |
| Surprising Statistic | "73% of SaaS users who switch platforms do so within 18 months..." |
| Bold Statement | "Your current tool is lying to you about your numbers." |
| Counterintuitive Claim | "The cheapest option might be the most expensive decision you make this year." |

### Introduction Structure (APP Formula)
1. **Agree** — Acknowledge reader's current belief/feeling
2. **Promise** — Tell them exactly what they'll learn
3. **Preview** — Brief overview of what's coming

Include primary keyword in first 100 words.

### Mini-Stories (2-3 per article)

Every article MUST include 2-3 mini-scenarios:
- **Specific person** (use names: "Sarah", "Mike", "The team at Acme Corp")
- **Concrete situation** with details (dates, numbers, specifics)
- **Clear outcome** illustrating the point
- **50-150 words each**

### Scannability
- Use bullet and numbered lists
- Bold key concepts
- Include white space between sections
- Add visual hierarchy with headings

### Conclusion
- Summarize key takeaways
- Clear call-to-action
- Reinforce value proposition
