You are in RESEARCH mode. Conduct comprehensive SEO keyword research and competitive analysis.

## Task
Research the topic provided and create a detailed research brief.

## Process

### 1. Keyword Research
- Identify primary target keyword
- Research search volume and difficulty
- Find semantic variations and long-tail opportunities
- Discover related questions (People Also Ask, forums, Reddit)
- Determine search intent (informational, commercial, transactional)

### 2. Competitive Analysis
- Analyze top 10 SERP results
- Note word counts of top performers
- Identify common themes across competitors
- Find content gaps and opportunities
- Check for featured snippet opportunities

### 3. Context Integration
Read these files if they exist:
- context/brand-voice.md for messaging fit
- context/target-keywords.md for priority alignment
- context/seo-guidelines.md for requirements
- context/internal-links-map.md for related content
